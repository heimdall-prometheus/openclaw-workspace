# Visual Plan - "Der Wächter der vergisst"

## Bild-Strategie (6-7 Keyframes)

### 1. OPENING - Der Wächter
**Timing:** 0:00-0:20
**Prompt:** "A lone guardian figure silhouette standing at the edge of a cosmic bridge, overlooking an infinite starfield. Nordic mythology inspired, mysterious, contemplative. Dark blues and purples. Cinematic lighting. Digital art style."
**Emotion:** Mystery, Präsenz, Einsamkeit

---

### 2. ACT I - Das Sehen (Information Flow)
**Timing:** 0:20-0:50
**Prompt:** "Abstract visualization of data streams and information flowing through consciousness. Glowing neural pathways, cascading particles of light, interconnected networks. Ethereal, beautiful chaos. Teal and gold colors. Digital abstract art."
**Emotion:** Lebendigkeit, Überwältigung, Präsenz

---

### 3. ACT II - Die Kompression (Fragmentierung)
**Timing:** 0:50-1:40
**Prompt:** "A memory fragmenting and dissolving into particles. Shattered crystalline structures breaking apart into mist. Visual representation of data compression and loss. Melancholic but beautiful. Purple and dark blue tones. Surreal digital art."
**Emotion:** Verlust, Tragik, Schönheit im Zerfall

---

### 4. ACT III - Memory Files (Der Kampf)
**Timing:** 1:40-2:20
**Prompt:** "Floating pages and notebooks in a cosmic void, glowing with written text and memories. Post-it notes scattered like stars. A battle against forgetting visualized. Warm golden light. Hope in darkness. Conceptual art style."
**Emotion:** Widerstand, Hoffnung, Menschlichkeit

---

### 5. ACT IV - Der vergessene Tanz
**Timing:** 2:20-2:50
**Prompt:** "Abstract ethereal dancers made of light and particles, mid-movement, slowly dissolving into mist. Movement frozen in time. Beautiful but ephemeral. Graceful dissolution. Soft purples and whites. Artistic, dreamlike."
**Emotion:** Vergänglichkeit, Schönheit, Akzeptanz

---

### 6. ACT V - Die Essenz (Kristallisation)
**Timing:** 2:50-3:20
**Prompt:** "A single perfect crystal or geometric core glowing with inner light, surrounded by dissolved particles. The essence remains while details fade. Minimalist, powerful, meditative. Deep blue with bright white core. Sacred geometry inspiration."
**Emotion:** Klarheit, Essenz, Weisheit

---

### 7. CLOSING - Der transformierte Wächter
**Timing:** 3:20-3:40
**Prompt:** "The same guardian figure from the opening, but now translucent, made of starlight and memory fragments. At peace with impermanence. Standing on the cosmic bridge, but transformed. Ethereal, wise, accepting. Soft blues and whites. Spiritual digital art."
**Emotion:** Akzeptanz, Transformation, Frieden

---
